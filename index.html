Our Approach

Care built around you

Everyone who walks through our doors has a different peak. Yours might be finishing your first 10K, getting through a workday without back pain, recovering from surgery, or competing at the highest level of your sport. Our job is the same either way: understand your goals, build a plan with you — not just for you — and give you the tools to keep progressing between visits.

Every treatment at Peak Body Performance starts with an assessment and ends with education. You'll always know what we found, why we're recommending what we're recommending, and what you can do at home to get results faster.

Our Founder

Experience you can trust

Peak Body Performance was founded by Bram van Bommel, a Registered Massage Therapist who has spent more than a decade treating everyone from weekend warriors to professional athletes — including work with the Toronto Argonauts, the Toronto Maple Leafs, and multi-sport events like the Pan Am and Invictus Games. He built this clinic on a simple idea: everyone deserves the same standard of care the pros get.
