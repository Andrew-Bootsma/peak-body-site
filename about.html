About Us

Built to help you reach your peak.

Our story

Peak Body Performance was created with a simple vision: to provide exceptional patient-centered care focused on helping people move better, recover faster, and perform at their peak. We're a welcoming environment where individuals and families can access high-quality healthcare and receive the personalized care they deserve — whatever their age, goals, or starting point.

Our vision

Founder Bram van Bommel spent over a decade working across clinics, championship teams, and international games — and kept seeing the same thing: the best outcomes happen when practitioners collaborate, when patients are educated partners in their own care, and when treatment is measured against personal goals instead of generic protocols.

Peak Body Performance brings that model to Oshawa. We're opening with Registered Massage Therapy and Naturopathic Medicine, with chiropractic care, physiotherapy, and mental health services joining as we grow — a team of regulated professionals working together, around you.

What we believe

Patient-focused.

Your goals drive the plan. Full stop.

Collaborative.

Between you and your practitioner, and between the practitioners on your team.

For everyone.

Peak performance isn't just for pros. Everyone has a peak worth pursuing.

Our Founder

Bram van Bommel, RMT

A Registered Massage Therapist since 2014, Bram has treated everyone from weekend warriors to professional athletes — including work with the Toronto Argonauts, the Toronto Maple Leafs, the Pan Am and Invictus Games, and the National Bank Open. He founded Peak Body Performance to bring that standard of collaborative, goals-first care home to Oshawa.
