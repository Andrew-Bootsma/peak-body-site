Our Services

One clinic. A team approach to your health.

Peak Body Performance brings multiple disciplines under one roof so your care can be coordinated instead of scattered. We're opening with Registered Massage Therapy and Naturopathic Medicine, and adding new services as we grow. If we believe a different type of care would serve you better — inside our clinic or beyond it — we'll tell you, and we'll help you get there.

Massage Therapy

Our Registered Massage Therapists (RMTs) are regulated by the College of Massage Therapists of Ontario and provide assessment-based treatment for:

  • Sports injuries, pre- and post-event preparation and recovery
  • Chronic muscle tension, headaches, and postural strain
  • Rehabilitation after injury or surgery
  • Pregnancy-related discomfort
  • General maintenance and relaxation

Techniques include deep tissue work, trigger point therapy, myofascial release, joint mobilizations, PNF and passive stretching, manual lymphatic drainage, and athletic/kinesiology taping. Your treatment always begins with a conversation about your goals and an assessment — the plan is built from what we find, not from a menu.

Naturopathic Medicine

Our Naturopathic Doctors (NDs) are licensed by the College of Naturopaths of Ontario and take a whole-person approach: understanding the root causes behind your symptoms and building a plan that fits your life. Naturopathic care at Peak Body Performance can support:

  • Digestive health and food sensitivities
  • Energy, sleep, and stress
  • Hormonal health
  • Sports performance and recovery nutrition
  • Preventive health and healthy aging

Your first visit is a comprehensive intake — expect a real conversation about your health history, lifestyle, and goals, followed by an individualized plan that may include nutrition, lifestyle counselling, supplementation, and other evidence-informed therapies.
